## What plans are available?

- **Free** — Forever free, no credit card required. 100k welcome tokens, up to 2 Spaces, up to 30,000 characters per upload. Smart compression, anonymize data, merge conversations.
- **Pro** — $9.90/month. 1M monthly tokens (non-expiring), unlimited Spaces, no upload limit. Higher API rate limits, priority support, semantic compression.
- **Enterprise** — Custom pricing. SSO, private deployments, SCIM, dedicated account manager, SLA.

## How do tokens work?

New accounts receive a one-time grant of **100k welcome tokens** to try the service. Pro subscribers get **1M tokens per month** (non-expiring). Additional tokens are sold as packs: 1M for $4.90, 5M for $19.90, 10M for $29.90. Token prices are subject to change with at least 30 days’ notice.

## Is there a free trial?

Yes. The Free plan is free forever—no credit card required. New accounts get 100k welcome tokens to try the service. You can purchase additional token packs anytime without upgrading.

## When do I need API access?

The API is available on **Pro and Enterprise** plans. Use it for automation, bulk processing, and integration with your own tools. Free users can use the web app with copy-paste workflow.
